ABOUT
FOUNDED: 2017
INSTAGRAM: HACKSTREET
FROM: NEW YORK CITY

Founded in 2017, Hackstreet was directed to represent, provide guidance and inform news for anyone who is interested in men’s contemporary street fashion.
Men’s contemporary street fashion inclluded in Hackstreet is heavily based off of men’s(Celeberities, students, models, basically anyone) trendy fashion spotted in the streets of New York City.

Hackstreet does not directly sell or purchase goods to/from the users. However, it suggests where a user can purchase a certain item; either online or offline(within New York City).

Hackstreet is updated every week, featuring various/reoccurent brands or style in contemporary men’s street fashion.

With Hackstreet, users will easily gain information and have raised awareness about street fashion as a whole.
