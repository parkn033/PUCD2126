20 years old, currently living in New York, New York. Attending Parsons The New School for Design. Grew up and spent most of my life back in the motherland, Seoul, South Korea. Attended Kyeseong Elementry School for 4 years, then transferred to Korea International School as a 4th grader then continued education there all the way through High-School graduation. Now that I am here in New York, which is on the other side of the globe compared to my motherland, I seek for any teachers/aquintances/friends/haters who have supported and shaped me to a person I am today.
