sound cloud

Product Objectives

  1. Provide music for users

  2. Analyze users' taste in music and recommends tracks

  3. Allow users to create their own personal playlists

  4. Encourages users to not only listen to various tracks, but also post their own tracks

Audience Segments

  1. Teenagers with Smartphones

  2. Adults with Smartphones

  3. People who enjoy listening to music

  4. People willing to share their playlists and tracks

  5. People who are unwilling to download music directly to their phones

Audience Needs

  1. Various tracks instantly available to listen to

  2. Solid recommendations of tracks based on their taste in music

  3. Achieving feedback on their tracks from other users
